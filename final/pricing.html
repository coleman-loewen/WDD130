pricing

Staff seminars

Our training seminars typically take 2-3 hours. It will be a crash course in physical and cyber security, topics discussed will include password security, social engineering, phishing, open source intelligence, and a myriad of other threats to your company security. Your staff will learn how to recognize and respond to these different threats. Pricing starts at $50 per person, one on one training is also available for $100 per hour.

Red team engagement

With your permission we will take on the role of a cyber criminal and attempt to "break into" your company. It will be a combination of physical and digital attacks to search for "red flags": vulnerabilites that hackers could exploit. When the attack is finished we will give you an accurate and detailed report of every problem we found along with our reccommendations on how to best fix it. The baseline price of our red team begins at $40,000. Prices are liable to change based on the length and scope of the target.
